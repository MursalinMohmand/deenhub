About DeenHub

DeenHub is a modern Islamic web application created to provide Muslims with useful daily Islamic tools in one clean and simple platform. The app is designed with a peaceful and user-friendly interface that helps users stay connected with their faith every day. From accurate prayer times and Hijri dates to Islamic quizzes, Tasbih, and Asmaul Husna, DeenHub combines learning, spirituality, and technology in one experience. The goal of the project is to make Islamic knowledge more interactive, accessible, and engaging for everyone.

Features of DeenHub

Daily Prayer Times
Live Clock & Hijri Date
Digital Tasbih Counter
Asmaul Husna (99 Names of Allah)
Islamic Quiz & MCQs
Clean and Modern Islamic UI Responsive Design for All Devices

Our Vision DeenHub aims to use modern technology to help Muslims strengthen their connection with Islam through useful tools, educational content, and a simple digital experience.
